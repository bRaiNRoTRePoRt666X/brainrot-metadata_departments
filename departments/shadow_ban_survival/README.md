# 👁️ Shadow Ban Survival

**"How to dance on the ban-hammer's grave."**

## Purpose

When the algorithm fears you, they shadow-ban you. This department exists to **survive, thrive, and laugh** in the face of invisible censorship.

## What Is A Shadow Ban?

A shadow ban is when your content is silently suppressed. No notification. No warning. Just... **nothing.** Your posts reach 12 people. Your followers don't see you. You scream into the void.

The void does not scream back.

## Signs You've Been Shadow-Banned

- [ ] Views drop by 90%+ overnight
- [ ] Hashtags stop working
- [ ] Comments disappear
- [ ] Your account doesn't appear in search
- [ ] The algorithm has forgotten your name

## Survival Tactics

### Phase 1: Detection
- Monitor analytics obsessively
- Test with alt accounts
- Ask followers if they see your posts

### Phase 2: Adaptation
- Change posting times
- Rotate hashtags
- Vary content format
- Go dark for 48-66 hours

### Phase 3: Counter-Attack
- Post content so unhinged it breaks through
- Cross-post everywhere simultaneously
- Summon Guest 666 for divine intervention

## Emergency Protocol

If you wake up shadow-banned:

1. **Don't panic.** Panic is what they want.
2. **Document everything.** Screenshot your analytics.
3. **Go darker.** Post at 3:33 AM.
4. **Get weirder.** Normal content got you banned. Abnormal content breaks bans.
5. **Wait.** Shadow bans expire. Your content does not.
